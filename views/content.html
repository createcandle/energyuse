If you don't have any energy monitoring devices yet, we recommend the Heiman (Zigbee) smart plug, which you can find on websites such as Aliexpress.

If you already use such devices, then please wait a while. This addon checks and stores the values at midnight.

Please generate an authorization token

This addon requires an "authorizion token" to work. Without it, the data from your devices cannot be read.

You can generate this "password" by going to settings, developer, and then "create local authorization". There you can click the button to generate a password. Copy it, and paste it into the appropriate field in this addon's settings page. Then click 'apply' to save the token.

Debugging is enabled

This addon is currently set to save detailed information about its operation in the internal logs. This may impact your privacy, as this also means that insights into your behaviour might be stored in those logs.

You can disable debugging mode by going to Settings, then Addons, and then clicking on the Settings button for the Zibgbee2MQTT addon. There you can uncheck the debugging option and click Apply.
