Ignored devices

You may want some energy measurement devices to be skipped when calculating total use. Typically this happens when you have an energy measurement device that is plugged into another energy measurement device.

Details about the device will still be shown; only the calculation of total energy consumption values is affected.

Virtual devices

Some always-on devices, such as your internet router, consume a consistent and predictable amount of electricity every day.

To use smart plugs to measure their consumption would be a bit of a waste. Once you know how much energy a device normally uses, you can create a 'virtual energy measurement devices' instead. Doing so allows you to pretend that the energy consumption of the device is being measured without needing an real electricity measurement plug.

To add a virtual device, simply provide a name and how much kWh it uses per day.

This addon requires your permission to read devices

Use the Candle app store and open this addon's settings page. There you can give permission. Don't forget to click 'save' once you changes the permission.



If you don't have any energy monitoring devices yet, take a look at the recommended devices on www.candlesmarthome.com.

If you already use such devices, then please wait a while. This addon checks and stores the values at midnight.

Debugging is enabled

This addon is currently set to save detailed information about its operation in the internal logs. This may impact your privacy, as this also means that insights into your behaviour might be stored in those logs.

You can disable debugging mode by going to Settings, then Addons, and then clicking on the Settings button for the Zibgbee2MQTT addon. There you can uncheck the debugging option and click Apply.
